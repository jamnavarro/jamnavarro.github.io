My role

I designed the UI and quest system design in the KC-based entertainment art experience, Atlas9.

Atlas9 is a narrative-driven art experience set in a 90s movie theater where visitors, as "secret agents", explore the mystery of film characters coming to life. Working with a senior developer, I designed the digital user interface for these "agent"-facing interactives.
