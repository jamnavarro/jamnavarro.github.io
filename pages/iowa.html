"How might we bring joy in undergraduate student housing?"
PROJECT OVERVIEW
University of Iowa sought to implement a digital activation in the lounge area of one of their residence and dining halls.
In summer 2023, during my stint as a UI/UX Design Intern with Dimensional Innovations, I had the opportunity to work on the mobile component of an interactive digital experience for a dormitory lounge in the University of Iowa.
MY ROLE
I brought the UX user flow to life by designing the web-app's UI design. I also streamlined Dimensional Innovations' UX process by implementing the company's style guide templates, with Iowa's digital ecosystem project being the first to be implemented with such templates.
