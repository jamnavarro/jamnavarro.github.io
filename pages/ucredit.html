Discovering the context
uCredit is a four-year degree planning platform to help with undergraduate degree progress and course selection.
Its main features include a planning dashboard and a reviewer dashboard. From previous heuristic evaluation data, working on improving the reviewer dashboard was an important step for the platform's future.
I worked with one partner for this project. Together, we recruited, conducted, and took notes for user interviews. My personal work included processing the interview research data and conducting competitive research from various direct competitors.
